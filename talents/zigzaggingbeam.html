Zigzagging Lasers


Prerequisite:

  • 3. Prime Level
  • Heat Beam I. feat

Description:

You lasers can change directions instantly mid-air, which makes easier to hit opponents.

  • Your Heat Beam ignore covers.
  • You can change the direction of your Heat Beam multiple times during your attack, but the lasers can not move more than 60 feet, and you still have to see your target.
