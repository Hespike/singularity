Heat Vision II


Prerequisite:

  • 5. Prime Level
  • Heat Beam talent

Description:

As you are becoming more experienced, your lasers are becoming stronger too.

  • Your Heat Vision cantrip deals 2d10 damage.

Heat Vision II

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Target: A creature or object within range
Components: S
Duration: Instantaneous
You send lasers from your eye at a creature or object within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 2d20 fire damage. A flammable object hit by this spell ignites if it isn’t being worn or carried.
