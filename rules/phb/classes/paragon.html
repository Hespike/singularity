Paragon

A superhero flies through the sky, reassuring everyone that they are safe.
The supervillain looks down on his subjects, reminding them that he sees everything and his iron fist reaches even the most hidden corners of the city.

Paragon is the most classic superhero. If you want your character to be like Superman, Homelander, Omni-Man or Shazam, then paragon is the right choice for you.

Hit Points

Your hit points equals (10 * your paragon level) + (endurance modifier * your paragon level)

Skills

Choose from 2 from acrobatics, athletics, intimidation, persuasion. You get a continuously scaling bonus equal to your level from these skills. So e.g. At level 1 you get +1 in addition to your Might modifier from the given skill, at level 10 you get +10 in addition to your Might modifier.

Features

Level 1

Flying
