Size

A kaiju is trying to destroy the city.
An alien, barely visible to the human eye, tries to avoid the bugs that appear to her to be giant.
The hero shrinks in size to fit through a crack in the wall and escape the villain's lair.

All PCs and NPCs have a size. This size can be a result of race, class ability or even a special item. The size of the character not only determines the size of its token, but also what rules apply to it. On this page you can find the most important information regarding the different sizes

Size categories (from smallest to largest)
