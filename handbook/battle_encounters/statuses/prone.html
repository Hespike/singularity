Prone

You are lying on the ground, either because you were knocked down, fell, or chose to drop. While Prone, your tactical options and mobility are severely limited.

Effects:

  • You cannot use the Move or Step actions. Your only movement option is to Crawl (moving at half your Speed).
  • You receive a -2 penalty to your AC against melee attacks.
  • You are a smaller target for those at a distance. You receive a +2 bonus to your AC against ranged attacks.
  • You receive a -2 penalty to all attack checks you make while Prone.
