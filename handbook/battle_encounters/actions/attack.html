Attack

Type: Action
Cost: Variable (See weapon or talent)

Description:
You engage an opponent directly using your equipped weaponry, specialized tools, or superhuman abilities to inflict damage or debuffs.

Notes:

  • Weapon and talent attacks use specific rules tailored to the equipment or power being used.
  • For detailed rules on accuracy, damage dice, and range, please refer to the Weapons page or the corresponding page for the specific talent you are using.
