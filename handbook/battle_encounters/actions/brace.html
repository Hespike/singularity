Brace

Type: Action
Cost: 1 energy

Description:
You tighten your stance, raise your guard, or focus your mind to weather an incoming threat.

You gain a +1 bonus to AC or a +2 bonus to one specific Saving Throw of your choice.

This bonus lasts until the start of your next turn. You must choose which bonus you are receiving at the moment you take this action.
