Blast (Apprentice)

Description:
You project a focused blast of destructive energy at a target.

Requirement:
Blast talent

Type: Ranged attack
Range: 30 feet
Cost: 2 energy
Hands: 0
Damage: 1d4 + chosen ability modifier (damage type chosen when you gained the Blast talent)

Rules:

  • Blast is treated as a ranged weapon attack for all rules, penalties, and interactions, but uses the ability modifier selected when you gained this talent for both attack and damage rolls. Also you can add your Blast competence level to the attack roll.
  • Blast does not require a physical weapon and cannot be disarmed.
