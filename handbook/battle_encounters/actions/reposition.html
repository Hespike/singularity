Reposition

Type: Action
Cost: 2 energy

Description:
You use your physical prowess or tactical leverage to force an opponent into a different position.

Check:
Might vs. the target's Might DC or Agility DC.

Notes:

  • Attacker: If you are trained in Athletics, you may use it for this check.
  • Defender: The target can use their Athletics DC or Acrobatics DC instead of their attribute DCs if it is higher.

Results:

  • Extreme Success: You move the target up to 10 feet in any direction and they become Prone.
  • Success: You move the target up to 5 feet in any direction.
  • Failure: The target holds their ground.
  • Extreme Failure: You lose your leverage and fall Prone in your current space.
