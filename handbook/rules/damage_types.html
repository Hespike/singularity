Damage Types

Damage reduces HP (Hit Points). When a creature is dealt damage, apply the following modifiers based on their properties:

  • Immunity: The creature cannot be hurt by the specified type of damage.
  • Resistance: Subtract the resistance value from the total damage dealt (e.g., Resistance 5 means -5 damage).
  • Weakness: Increase the damage dealt by the weakness value (e.g., Weakness 5 means +5 damage).

All damage modifiers (Immunity, Resistance, and Weakness) are applied after the final damage is calculated, including any doublings or bonuses from critical hits.

PsychicMental assaults that damage the mind, causing confusion, pain, or trauma.
SonicVibrations and sound waves that deafen, disrupt, or shatter.
RadiantPurifying or divine energy that burns away darkness and corruption.
ChaosUnstable magical or cosmic forces that twist reality or wreak havoc unpredictably.
