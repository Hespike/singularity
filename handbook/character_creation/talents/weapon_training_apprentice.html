Weapon Training (Apprentice)

Type: Talent (Weapon)

Description

Your character improves their skill with weapons through training and practice. By focusing on a single weapon category, you can increase your training level and perform better in combat with those weapons.

Effect

Choose one weapon category in which your character is currently Novice. Your training level in that category increases to Apprentice. You may use the table below as guidance for weapon categories and examples:

Weapon Category Examples
Unarmed Strikes Fists, Kicks, Natural Weapons
Light Melee Weapons Daggers, Short Swords, Tonfas
Heavy Melee Weapons Greatswords, Hammers, Polearms
Thrown Weapons Throwing Knives, Javelins, Shurikens
Bows Longbow, Shortbow, Composite Bow
Firearms Pistols, Rifles, Shotguns
Improvised Weapons Rocks, Chairs, Tools

Notes

  • If you have training in Light Melee Weapons, the bonus applies only when the weapon is used in melee. Throwing the weapon does not grant the bonus.
  • If you have training in Thrown Weapons, the bonus applies only when the weapon is thrown. Using a thrown weapon in melee does not grant the bonus.
