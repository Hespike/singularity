Weapon Training (Apprentice)

Type: Talent (Weapon)

Requirements

  • Character Level 1

Action type

Passive

Effect

Choose one weapon category in which your character is currently Novice. Your proficiency in that category increases to Apprentice. You may use the table below as guidance for weapon categories and examples:

Weapon Category Examples
Unarmed Strikes Fists, Kicks, Natural Weapons
Light Melee Weapons Daggers, Short Swords, Tonfas
Heavy Melee Weapons Greatswords, Hammers, Polearms
Thrown Weapons Throwing Knives, Javelins, Shurikens
Bows Longbow, Shortbow, Composite Bow
Firearms Pistols, Rifles, Shotguns
Improvised Weapons Rocks, Chairs, Tools
