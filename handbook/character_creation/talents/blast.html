Blast

Type: Talent (Generic)

Description

You unleash a focused burst of destructive energy, shaped by your chosen talent and technique. This blast may manifest as elemental force, technological firepower, or raw supernatural power, depending on how you wield it.

Effect

When you gain this talent, choose a damage type and an ability score. These choices define how your Blast functions and what fuels its power.

You can use the Blast (Apprentice) action.

Ability Used: Might, Agility, Wits, or Charm

Damage Type: See Damage Types
