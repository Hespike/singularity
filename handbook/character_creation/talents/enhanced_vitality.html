Enhanced Vitality

Type: Talent (Resilience)

Description

Your hero is built tougher than most, with greater reserves of stamina and durability. This extra vitality allows them to withstand significantly more damage over the course of their adventures.

Effect

Your hit point maximum increases by an amount equal to twice your Prime Level.
Whenever you gain a level thereafter, your hit point maximum increases by an additional 2 hit points.
