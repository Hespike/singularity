Criminal

Description

You lived outside the law, relying on quick thinking, steady nerves, and knowing when to disappear. Whether as a thief, smuggler, fixer, or gang member, survival meant skill and adaptability.

Benefits

  • Ability Boost: Increase your Agility, Endurance, or Charm ability score by +2 (choose one).
  • Street Skills: You gain Apprentice training in Stealth(Agility), Deception(Charm), or Survival(Wits) (choose one).
