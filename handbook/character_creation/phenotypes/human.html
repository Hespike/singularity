Human

Description

Humans are versatile and adaptable, capable of thriving in nearly any environment or profession. Their ingenuity and resourcefulness make them quick learners and flexible adventurers.

Benefits

  • Base speed: 25 feet
  • Size: Medium
  • Ability Boost: +1 to any one ability of your choice
  • Starting Talent: 1 free talent of your choice at 1st level

Subtypes

  • Terran
