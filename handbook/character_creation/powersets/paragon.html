Paragon

The Paragon is the embodiment of overwhelming physical supremacy. These heroes soar through the skies, endure catastrophic force, and strike with raw power that shatters steel and stone alike. Whether born superhuman, empowered by alien energy, or transformed by Singularium, Paragons dominate the battlefield through sheer presence and unstoppable momentum.

Paragons do not rely on armor, tactics, or preparation. Their bodies are the weapon, the shield, and the symbol.

Example heroes:

  • Superman (DC Comics) - A near-invulnerable flying powerhouse, defined by strength, endurance, and moral resolve.
  • Omni-Man (Invincible) - A brutal paragon of flight and overwhelming force.
  • Homelander (The Boys) - A terrifying example of unchecked power and dominance.
