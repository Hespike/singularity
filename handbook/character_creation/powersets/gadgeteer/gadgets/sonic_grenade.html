Sonic Grenade

Type: Crowd Control / Defense

Range: 10-foot burst (centered on you)

Cost: 4 energy

Hands: 0 (Integrated)

Duration: 1 round

Description

You trigger a high-intensity discharge from a specialized canister or chest-plate, releasing a blinding magnesium flash and a disorienting 160-decibel sonic boom.

Effect

All creatures other than you within the radius must make an Agility saving throw against your Gadget Tuning DC. The effect depends on their result:

  • Extreme Success: The creature is unaffected.
  • Success: The creature is Dazed until the start of your next turn.
  • Failure: The creature is Staggered 1 and Dazed until the start of your next turn.
  • Extreme Failure: The creature is Stunned 2, Dazed, and Deafened until the start of your next turn.
