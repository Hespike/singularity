Support Drone

Description: You deploy a micro-drone that hovers near an ally, providing real-time tactical overlays and sensor data to optimize their next move.

Type: Utility / Support

Range: 30 feet

Cost: 1 energy

Hands: 1 (to deploy)

Duration: Until the start of your next turn

Rules

The target gains a +1 bonus to one attack roll, saving throw, or skill check they attempt before the duration ends. The target chooses which roll to use the bonus on before rolling. If the target uses the bonus, the effect ends.

Once a creature has benefited from the Support Drone, they become temporarily immune to this gadget for 10 minutes.
