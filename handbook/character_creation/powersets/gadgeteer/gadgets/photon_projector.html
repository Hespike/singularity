Photon Projector

Description: You activate a compact, high-intensity LED device or launch a sticky "flare" bead that illuminates the surrounding area with steady, flicker-free light.

Type: Utility

Range: 30 feet

Cost: 1 energy

Hands: 1 (to activate)

Duration: 1 hour

Rules

The projector emits bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for an additional 20 feet.
