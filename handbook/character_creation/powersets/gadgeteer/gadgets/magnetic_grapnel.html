Magnetic Grapnel

Description: You fire a high-tension, motorized cable with a multi-purpose magnetic tip that anchors to surfaces, allowing for rapid vertical or horizontal repositioning.

Type: Movement / Utility

Range: 30 feet

Cost: 2 energy

Hands: 1 (to aim and fire)

Duration: Instantaneous

Rules

Target a fixed point or a surface within range. You are immediately pulled to that location.

Notes

  • Because this movement is mechanical and sudden, it does not trigger the Tactical Withdrawal penalty, allowing you to move away from melee enemies at your full distance.
