Liquid Foam Spray

Description: You unleash a pressurized canister of quick-hardening chemical foam that coats the ground or an enemy's limbs, making movement extremely difficult.

Type: Utility / Crowd Control

Range: 15 feet

Cost: 2 energy

Hands: 1 (to spray)

Duration: 10 minutes

Rules

You target a 5-foot square. That square becomes difficult terrain.

Notes

This penalty stacks with the Tactical Withdrawal rule. If a creature attempts to move away from an enemy while inside the foam, their speed is effectively reduced to one-quarter.
