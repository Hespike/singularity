Bastion’s Resistance

Type: Talent (Bastion)

Requirements

  • Bastion 1

Action type: Passive

Your body (through training, mutation, armor, or sheer will) is built to endure punishment.

Effect

Choose one damage type when you take this talent. You gain resistance to the chosen damage type equal to 2 × your Bulwark level.

You can take this talent multiple times, choosing a different damage type each time.
