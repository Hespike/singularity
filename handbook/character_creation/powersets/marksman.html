Marksman

The Marksman is a master of precision combat, relying on skill, focus, and timing rather than raw power. With a steady hand and sharp eyes, Marksmen excel at taking out targets from a distance, controlling the battlefield, and exploiting weaknesses in their enemies’ defenses.

Example Heroes

  • Deadshot (DC Comics) – A literal living weapon capable of near-perfect accuracy.
  • Bullseye (Marvel Comics) – Turns anything into a lethal projectile.
  • Hawkeye (Marvel Comics) – A tactical archer combining agility and precision.

Class Statistics

HP: Your HP at each level is equal to

(6 + your Endurance modifier) × your Marksman level

Marksman Progression

Level 1

  • Agility Boost: +2 to your Agility ability.
  • Ability Boost: +2 to any one ability of your choice.
  • Skill Training: You gain Apprentice training in one skill of your choice.
  • Ranged Weapon Competence: You are Apprentice with all ranged weapons.
  • Marksman Talent: You can choose 1 Marksman talent.
    Marksman talents

Level 5

  • Ranged Weapon Competence: You are Competent with all ranged weapons.

Level 10

  • Ranged Weapon Competence: You are Masterful with all ranged weapons.

Level 15

  • Ranged Weapon Competence: You are Legendary with all ranged weapons.
