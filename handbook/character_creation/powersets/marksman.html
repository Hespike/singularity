Marksman

The Marksman is a master of precision combat, relying on skill, focus, and timing rather than raw power. With a steady hand and sharp eyes, Marksmen excel at taking out targets from a distance, and exploiting weaknesses in their enemies’ defenses.

Example Heroes

  • Deadshot (DC Comics) – A literal living weapon, Deadshot can hit almost any target with deadly accuracy, often using trick shots and strategic positioning.
  • Bullseye (Marvel Comics) – Known for turning anything into a lethal projectile, his precision and creativity make him a feared Marksman.
  • Hawkeye (Marvel Comics) – A master archer who combines agility, precision, and tactical thinking to outmaneuver opponents.

Hit Points

HP: Your HP at each level is equal to (6 + your Endurance) × your Marksman level.

Marksman benefits

Level 1

  • Agility boost: +1 to your Agility ability.
  • Ability boost: +1 to any one ability of your choice.
  • Skill training: You gain Apprentice training with one skill of your choice.
  • Ranged Weapon Competence: You are Apprentice with all ranged weapons.
  • Marksman talent: You can choose 1 Marksman talent.
    Marksman talents

Level 5

  • Ranged Weapon Competence: You are Competent with all ranged weapons.

Level 10

  • Ranged Weapon Competence: You are Masterful with all ranged weapons.

Level 15

  • Ranged Weapon Competence: You are Legendary with all ranged weapons.
