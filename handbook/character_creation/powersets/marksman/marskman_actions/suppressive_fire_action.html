Suppressive Fire

Requirements

  • Marksman 1
  • Suppressive Fire talent
  • You wield a loaded ranged weapon

Action Type

Action (Cost: 2 energy)

Target

One enemy within your weapon's first range increment.

Description

Your shots keep enemies pinned down, limiting their ability to retaliate effectively.

Effect

  • Make a ranged Strike to use Suppressive Fire, expending 1 ammo.
  • Extreme Success: The target takes a -6 penalty on its next attack roll.
  • Success: The target takes a -3 penalty on its next attack roll.
  • Failure: Nothing happens.
  • Extreme Failure: Your shots go wide, revealing your position. The target gains a +2 bonus on its next attack roll against you.
