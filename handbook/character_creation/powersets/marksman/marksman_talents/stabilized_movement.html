Stabilized Movement

Type: Talent (Marksman)

Requirements

  • Marksman 3
  • Deadeye

Action Type

Passive

Description

Your posture and balance keep your weapon steady, even on the move.

Effect

When you use Deadeye, you do not lose its attack bonus if you move before making the attack. You still lose the bonus if the target moves.
