Deadeye

Type: Talent (Marksman)

Requirements

  • Marksman 1
  • You wield a ranged weapon

Action Type

Action (Cost: 2 Energy)

Target

One enemy you can see.

Description

You steady your weapon with razor focus, lining up the perfect shot.

Effect

You may spend an action to carefully aim. Your next attack with the ranged weapon you are wielding gains a +2 bonus to the attack roll.

If you or your target moves before the attack is made, you lose this bonus.
