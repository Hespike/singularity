Bastion

The Bastion is a living bulwark, standing between danger and those they protect. Through sheer resilience, superior protection, or superhuman toughness, Guardians absorb punishment that would fell others and hold the line when all else fails.

Whether clad in advanced armor, wielding an indestructible shield, or possessing an unbreakable body, Guardians define defense through endurance and control.

Example Heroes

  • Captain America (Marvel Comics) – A tactical defender who uses positioning, shield mastery, and resolve to protect allies.
  • The Thing (Marvel Comics) – A powerhouse of raw durability, shrugging off blows through sheer toughness.
  • Luke Cage (Marvel Comics) – Nearly impervious skin allows him to walk through gunfire and stand firm against overwhelming force.

Hit Points

HP: Your HP at each level is equal to

(12 + your Endurance modifier) × your Bastion level

Bastion Progression

Level 1

  • Endurance Boost: +2 to your Endurance ability.
  • Ability Boost: +2 to any one ability of your choice.
  • Armor Training: You are trained in Heavy Armor.
  • Defensive Bonus: You gain a +2 bonus to AC.
  • Bulwark Talent: You can choose 1 Bulwark talent.
    Bastion talents

Level 5

  • Defensive Bonus: Your bonus to AC increases to +4.

Level 10

  • Defensive Bonus: Your bonus to AC increases to +6.

Level 15

  • Defensive Bonus: Your bonus to AC increases to +8.

Level 20

  • Defensive Bonus: Your bonus to AC increases to +10.
