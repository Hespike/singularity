Gadgeteer

The Gadgeteer is the ultimate problem-solver, relying on intellect, innovation, and a vast arsenal of high-tech devices. While they may lack innate superpowers, their ability to adapt to any situation makes them indispensable.

Whether deploying drones, hacking security systems, or utilizing experimental weaponry, the Gadgeteer always has the right tool for the job.

Example Heroes

  • Batman (DC Comics) – The iconic “prep-time” hero who uses a utility belt full of specialized gadgets.
  • Cyborg (DC Comics) – Uses integrated technology to adapt his body to the battlefield.

Class Statistics

HP: Your HP at each level is equal to

(6 + your Endurance modifier) × your Gadgeteer level

Gadgeteer Progression

Level 1

  • Wits Boost: +2 to your Wits ability.
  • Ability Boost: +2 to any one ability of your choice.
  • Skill Training: Apprentice training in Electricity or Hacking (choose one).
  • Gadget Tuning: You gain Apprentice competence in Gadget Tuning (Wits). This ability is used for all checks made with your gadgets.

Prepared Gadgets

Gadgeteers prepare a limited number of gadgets in advance, representing pre-built devices, charged modules, and loaded systems.

After a long rest, you may change which gadgets you have prepared, choosing from any gadgets for which you have blueprints.

Gadget Slots (Level 1)

  • 4 Level 0 gadget slots
  • 2 Level 1 gadget slots

Using Gadgets

  • Level 0 gadgets can be used any number of times.
  • Level 1 gadgets can each be used once before needing re-preparation.
  • Each prepared gadget occupies one slot of its level.
  • You may prepare the same leveled gadget multiple timesin different slots.

Once a leveled gadget is used, its slot is expended and cannot be used again until after your next long rest.

Gadgets

  • Gadgeteer Talent: You can choose 1 Gadgeteer talent.
    Gadgeteer talents

Level 5

Level 10

Level 15
