Noisy

Description

This armor produces audible sounds when you move, such as clanking plates, shifting materials, or powered components. While wearing armor with the Noisy trait, you take a penalty equal to the listed value on sneaking or stealth checks.

Example

  • Noisy (1): –1 to stealth checks
  • Noisy (3): –3 to stealth checks
