Reload (Energy cost to reload / Shots)

Weapons with the Reload trait can only fire a limited number of times before needing to be reloaded. The first number indicates the energy cost to reload the weapon, and the second number indicates the number of shots restored per reload.

Rules

  • You cannot fire the weapon beyond the number of shots available without first performing a reload.
  • Reloading consumes the specified energy and replenishes the indicated number of shots.
  • The Reload trait is compatible with any weapon that has limited ammunition or magazine capacity.

Example

  • Reload (1 / 1): Costs 1 energy to reload, restores 1 shot.
  • Reload (2 / 6): Costs 2 energy to reload, restores 6 shots.
