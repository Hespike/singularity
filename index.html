New adventure awaits!

Check out the awesome adventure of the Black Winged Lord, where ancient dark forces gather in preparation for the cataclysm foretold before the world began. The mortals know little about the impending doom, but now, they must put forth the heroes that will stand up to the villains out of history.

Spies and Assassins

Secrecy and inrigue awaits the adventurers in the newly founded Commonwealth. Unknown forces work behind the curtains to wreak havok in a nation that seeks only peace after a long era of constant war. Will you step forth to protect the ideals of the common people, or will you seek glory and riches in the wars to come?
