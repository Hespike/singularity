Zigzagging Lasers


Prerequisite:

  • 3. Prime Level
  • Heat Vision feat

Description:

You lasers can change directions instantly mid-air, which makes easier to hit opponents.

  • Your Heat Vision ignore covers.
  • You can change the direction of your Heat Vision multiple times during your attack, but the lasers can not move more than 60 feet, and you still have to see your target.
